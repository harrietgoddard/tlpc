About Us

At The Loved Pot Co., we believe that the best cookware doesn't come brand new in a box. It's the pot with the worn wooden handle, the casserole that's cooked a hundred family meals, the copper pan with a rich patina and a past.

Ours is a slow, sustainable approach to cooking at home. We restore pre-owned kitchenware with care, breathing new life into beautiful old pieces made from timeless, durable materials. It's our way of celebrating quality, craftsmanship, and the quiet joy of things that are built to last.

What we do.

We specialise in sourcing and restoring second-hand kitchenware, with a particular focus on cast iron, copper, enamel and other enduring materials that are made to be used and loved for a lifetime.

Each item we sell is carefully inspected, cleaned and prepared for a new chapter in your kitchen. Here's how we work:

Condition checks:

We inspect every item for structural damage — such as cracks, chips in enamel, or warping — and check for safety concerns like the presence of lead in glazes or solder.

Thorough, non-toxic cleaning:

All pieces are deep cleaned using gentle but effective, non-toxic methods that are safe for cookware and for your home.

Re-seasoning:

For raw cast iron and carbon steel, we restore the cooking surface with a fresh layer of seasoning so it's ready to use.

Transparent listings:

We include detailed descriptions and high-quality images so you know exactly what you're getting. Any marks, signs of use or quirks are noted clearly.

Careful packaging:

Orders are packaged securely and thoughtfully. Each parcel includes a handwritten note with care instructions — and when we can, we'll share a little of the item's backstory too.
